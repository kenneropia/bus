How We Work

Intructions on how different segments of our service work

  • For Our Drivers

    You sign up and submit your driver license, then choose your preferred routes. Then you're automatically assigned, when the need arises.

  • For our Students

    You sign up, then choose your ride, if there is a driver available, an id is generated that you can use to board the bus
